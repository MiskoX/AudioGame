# 🎙️ Recorder App

**A simple, hilarious audio game you can play instantly in your browser.**

👉 [https://miskox.github.io/AudioGame/](https://miskox.github.io/AudioGame/)

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. 🎤 **Record** – Player A records a short phrase
2. 🔁 **Play reverse** – Player B listens to it backwards
3. 🎙️ **Imitate** – Player B records what they heard
4. 🔄 **Reveal** – Reverse the imitation
5. 😂 **Compare** – See how close it is

Repeat and switch roles.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎙️ Quick recording (up to 15s)
- 🔁 Reverse playback
- 📊 Simple timeline
- 🌗 Light / Dark mode
- 🌍 PL / EN

---

## 💡 Tips

- Use short, weird phrases
- Singing makes it funnier
- The more chaotic — the better 😄
